My school name is John Palmer Public School

Set within spacious, landscaped grounds, John Palmer Public School has an enrolment of approximately 960 students from K-6. Quality teaching and learning experiences provide the foundation for students to cooperate, succeed and grow as learners.

Spelling Bee

Spelling Bee

The Premier's Spelling Bee is an annual competition that encourages primary school students in Years 3 to 6 to develop their spelling skills in a fun and educational way. Students are selected at school level to take part in regional finals. Regional winners then compete in the state final.
